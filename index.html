What is ZSAT chess timer beta?

ZSAT chess timer is an app that helps you play chess with another person! Simple as that! It's a dynamic software. Basically, it's a mobile app that acts like a timer, but it's more than that. You can set a match timer limit and both the players have a total move time limit within that limit. Player one starts. After he moves, he presses the button. His timer pauses and player two's timer starts. When player two pauses, his timer resumes from where he stopped off at his last move. Pretty neat, huh? This is the idea behind the app. You will also be able to save matches with the match name, and each time you open the app, it asks you whether you want to start of from a previously saved match. 🤨 The app is still under devoloping. 😒 BUT, you can still use the app. 😏 This is where the "beta" comes in... In the beta version, we are not assuring you a bug-less glitch-less app. But you can still use th app with minimalistic features. In this particular beta version, you can only use the timer on-board. That is, if you close the app and open it again, it will be just like when you first used it. So basically, the app won't store anything, yet. Soon, in the next beta release, I will be adding the save match feature. Yay!
